Workflow example

A simple workflow sequence: verify a a user's identity, ask a question, request a credential, and issue a credential.

Connect the Organization ↔ Credential Holder
Provide context on the workflow Organization ↔ Credential Holder
Request a proof Organization ↔ Credential Holder
Send the proof Credential Holder ↔ Organization
Ask the questions Organization ↔ Credential Holder
Send the answer Credential Holder ↔ Organization
Offer the credential Organization ↔ Credential Holder
Accept the offer Credential Holder ↔ Organization
